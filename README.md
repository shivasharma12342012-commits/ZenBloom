## 💡 Why I Built This
Studying right now is harder than ever. We are constantly getting distracted by new tabs, social media, and notifications. Traditional timers are boring and feel like a chore. I wanted to build something that makes putting your phone away actually feel rewarding and peaceful. 

## 🚀 What is ZenBloom?
ZenBloom is a gamified study timer that lives right in your browser. When you start a 25-minute focus session, a tiny digital seed is planted on your screen. As you study, the seed grows into a massive tree. 

**But there is a catch!** ZenBloom has built-in tab-detection. If you get distracted and open another tab (like YouTube or Reddit), the webpage instantly knows! It pauses your timer and sends you a gentle warning to get back to work. Stay focused, grow your tree, and collect rewards.

## ✨ Cool Features
* **Anti-Distraction Technology:** Uses the browser's Page Visibility API to catch you if you leave the tab.
* **Visual Progress:** Watch your plant grow through 3 stages (Seed 🌰 -> Sprout 🌱 -> Tree 🌳) as time passes.
* **Instant Rewards:** Explodes with digital confetti when you finish a session.
* **Memory Saving:** Uses LocalStorage to save your "Focus Tokens" directly in your browser, so you never lose your score even if you refresh!

## 🛠️ How It Was Made
I wanted this app to be incredibly fast and run on any computer without needing complex backend servers.
* **HTML & JavaScript:** The bricks and electricity that make the timer tick.
* **Tailwind CSS:** For beautiful, relaxing, and modern colors.
* **Canvas-Confetti:** A special library just to make winning feel awesome.
* **SweetAlert2:** A library that turns ugly browser errors into beautiful warning pop-ups.

## 💻 Try It Yourself!
Because ZenBloom is entirely frontend-only, you don't need any servers to run it. 
1. Download the code folder.
2. Double-click `index.html`.
3. It opens in your browser instantly. Start growing!
